Western Michigan Plant Guide

Trees

Amelanchier canadensis (Serviceberry)

🌿⛅🦌

Native multi-stem tree with white spring flowers and edible berries. Great fall color.

Cornus florida (Flowering Dogwood)

⛅🦌

Spring blooms and brilliant red fall foliage.

Acer palmatum ‘Shaina’ (Dwarf Japanese Maple)

☁️🦌

Small tree with striking red foliage, perfect for shade gardens.

Acer ‘Crimson King’ (Red Maple cultivar)

☀️⛅

Vibrant red fall color, adaptable to sun or partial shade.

Acer ‘Bloodgood’ (Japanese Maple)

⛅

Dark red leaves, excellent for shade or partial sun.

Evergreens

Pinus strobus ‘Blue Shag’ (Dwarf Eastern White Pine)

🌲☀️🦌

Compact blue-green evergreen with soft needles.

Picea abies 'Pendula' (Weeping Norway Spruce)

🌲☀️

A choice evergreen with a naturally low growing form composed of weeping branches that trail outward, densely blanketed with rich green needles. Excellent when trained in a staked form to feature its naturally pendulous form, creating a dramatic evergreen garden specimen.

Blue Chip Juniper

🌲☀️🦌

Low-growing groundcover juniper with blue foliage.

Daub's Frosted Juniper

🌲☀️

Golden yellow, frosted foliage tips create a striking contrast to the pendulous, blue-green, mature foliage.

Buxus ‘Neatball’ (Boxwood)

🌲⛅🦌

Dense, rounded shrub, great for edging.

Drops of Gold Japanese Holly

🌲☁️

A uniquely variegated female holly with intriguing golden yellow highlights on the upper and outermost foliage that offer a striking contrast to the rich green leaf color of the lower and interior foliage.

Duke Gardens Japanese Plum Yew

🌲⛅

A graceful, finely textured evergreen shrub with a spreading, mounded habit. The dense, glossy, needle-like, foliage emerges bright green, then matures to a dark green and retains its color year-round.

Chamaecyparis obtusa ‘Hinoki’ (Hinoki Cypress)

🌲☁️

Ornamental cypress with unique texture and form.

Shrubs

Rosa rugosa

☀️🦌

Hardy shrub rose with fragrant blooms and red hips.

Berberis thunbergii ‘Crimson Pygmy’ (Crimson Barberry)

☀️🦌

Compact shrub with vibrant red leaves, thorny for deer resistance.

Hydrangea paniculata ‘Limelight’

☀️⛅

Large white blooms turning lime green, sun to partial shade.

Hydrangea arborescens (Mountain Hydrangea)

⛅🦌

Native hydrangea with large white flowers, shade tolerant.

Chamaecyparis obtusa ‘Hinoki’ (Hinoki Cypress)

🌲☁️

Ornamental cypress with unique texture and form.

Itea virginica ‘Little Henry’

⛅🦌

Compact shrub with fragrant white flowers and red fall color.

Rhododendron spp. (Azaleas & Rhododendrons)

⛅

Shade-loving flowering shrubs with showy spring blooms.

Cornus sericea (Red Twig Dogwood)

🌿☀️🦌

Deciduous shrub with striking red stems, winter interest.

Pieris japonica

🌲⛅

Evergreen shrub with bell-shaped flowers, partial shade.

Perennials

Echinacea purpurea (Purple Coneflower)

🌿☀️🦌

Long-blooming native with large purple flowers.

Rudbeckia fulgida (Black-eyed Susan)

🌿☀️🦌

Native bright yellow daisy-like flowers, summer-fall bloom.

Lavandula angustifolia (English Lavender)

☀️🌵🦌

Aromatic, drought tolerant, attracts pollinators.

Coreopsis verticillata (Tickseed)

☀️🦌

Bright yellow flowers, blooms summer to fall.

Nepeta faassenii (Catmint)

☀️⛅🦌

Aromatic foliage with blue flowers, deer resistant.

Salvia nemorosa (Wood Sage)

☀️🦌

Violet-blue spikes, attracts bees and butterflies.

Heuchera (Coral Bells)

⛅🦌

Colorful foliage from burgundy to lime green, shade tolerant.

Sedum spectabile (Showy Stonecrop)

☀️🦌

Succulent foliage with pink flower clusters in late summer.

Monarda didyma (Bee Balm)

☀️🦌

Red flowers attract hummingbirds and butterflies.

Asclepias tuberosa (Butterfly Weed)

☀️🦌

Bright orange flowers, excellent for pollinators.

Paeonia spp. (Peony)

☀️⛅🦌

Large, fragrant spring flowers in many colors.

Grasses & Ferns

Pennisetum alopecuroides (Fountain Grass)

☀️🦌

Elegant grass with arching foliage and fluffy plumes.

Calamagrostis x acutiflora ‘Karl Foerster’

☀️🦌

Tall, upright grass with golden plumes and great fall color.

Athyrium nipponicum (Japanese Painted Fern)

⛅🦌

Beautiful silver and green fern for shade gardens.

Bulbs

Tulipa spp. (Tulip)

☀️🦌

Popular spring bulbs in many colors and shapes.

Narcissus spp. (Daffodil)

☀️🦌

Early spring bloomers with bright yellow or white flowers.

Climbers & Vines

Clematis spp.

☀️⛅

Flowering vine with large, showy flowers in many colors.

Wisteria sinensis

☀️🦌

Fragrant cascading clusters of purple flowers.

Lonicera spp. (Honeysuckle)

☀️🦌

Sweetly fragrant, attracts pollinators.
