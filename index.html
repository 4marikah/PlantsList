Deer-Resistant Plants for Partial Shade

Buxus 'Neatball' (Boxwood)

Evergreen shrub perfect for edging and structure. Deer-resistant and winter-hardy.

Itea virginica 'Little Henry'

Compact shrub with fragrant white summer flowers and bright red fall foliage. Tolerates moist soils and shade.

Hydrangea paniculata 'Bobo'

Dwarf panicle hydrangea with abundant white blooms turning pink. Deer-tolerant and very showy.

Heuchera (Coral Bells)

Colorful foliage from burgundy to lime. A staple for shade gardens. Rarely browsed by deer.

Hakonechloa (Japanese Forest Grass)

Graceful, arching grass that adds motion and texture. Great for shady borders.

Nepeta (Catmint)

Blue-purple spires with aromatic foliage. Long blooming and deer-deterring.

Allium 'Millenium'

Summer-blooming ornamental onion with round purple flowers. Smell deters deer and rabbits.

Geranium 'Rozanne'

Vigorous blue-flowering perennial blooming all summer. Low maintenance and deer-resistant.
